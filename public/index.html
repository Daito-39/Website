Introduction

Hello and welcome to my little corner of the internet!

I'm Izzy, a 23 year old Comp Sci Uni grad looking to make an impact while sticking to my morals. I had attempted to make this site during my studies, although I was way too stressed to learn then. I've had a ton of time since to do so, and have been continuing to learn a bunch of languages.

This is a place for me to talk about things I've been doing, things I'm into and want to talk about, plus a way for me to experiment and test my coding abilities. I want to be writing a blog post each week, but sometimes I find myself coding more instead, so don't leave myself enough time to do so.

The site is still very much under construction, but making progress.

Feel free to take a look around, and leave a note in the guest book if you'd like to.

If you want to know more about me there is also the blog post below
