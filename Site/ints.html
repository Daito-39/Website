Work History

Film: In the academic year 2023/4 I was elected as vice chair of the University of Birmingham Film Society, which was an experience I loved, and allowed me to express my love for films with others. We ran meetings bi-weekly, showing a variety of films from across genres and countries, and also ran collaborations with other societies, including a fundraiser to raise money for the charity B:music.

Card & Board games: I enjoy playing card games, such as Magic: the gathering, where I enjoy playing formats such as draft, where you have to be creative and improvise, choosing the best cards you can to build the best deck, and then pilot it against other people. Additionally, I love commander, the multiplayer magic format, which is more of a board game, which i also love for their strategic aspects, as well as the community.

Art & Writing: In my spare time i try to make time to write and draw, as a creative outlet. I love to be able to express my creativity, through either clip studio paint or on paper for drawing, and writing whenever I feel inspired. I do also have experience with Adobe photoshop, and can work my way around it.
